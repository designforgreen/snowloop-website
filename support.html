❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📍 Why isn't my GPS tracking working?
Make sure Location Services are enabled for Snow Loop in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Snow Loop. Ensure you have good GPS signal and grant "Always" location access for optimal tracking performance on the slopes.
🌤️ Weather information not updating?
Weather data requires an active internet connection. Check your WiFi or cellular connection, then try refreshing by pulling down on the weather screen. If problems persist, restart the app or check for updates.
💾 How is my ski data stored and protected?
Your ski tracks are stored locally on your device and securely synced to our encrypted cloud servers. You maintain complete control over your data and can view, export, or delete it anytime through app settings.
🗺️ Can I use Snow Loop offline on the mountain?
Absolutely! Download maps for your favorite ski resorts before heading out. GPS tracking works completely offline, and your data will sync automatically when you're back online.
🏔️ Which Canadian ski resorts are supported?
Snow Loop provides comprehensive coverage of major Canadian ski destinations across British Columbia, Alberta, Ontario, and Quebec. We're continuously adding new resorts based on user requests.
📱 What are the system requirements?
Snow Loop requires iOS 15.0 or later and is optimized for iPhone. The app leverages the latest iOS features for superior performance, accuracy, and battery efficiency.
🔒 How do you protect my privacy?
Privacy is fundamental to Snow Loop. Your location data is encrypted end-to-end, never shared with advertisers, and you control what information is collected. Review our Privacy Policy for complete details.
